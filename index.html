New Business:


During the meeting, the team brainstormed several design ideas for the Pomodoro Timer and discussed the pros and cons of each option. After careful consideration, the group narrowed down the options to three potential designs. To move forward with the design process, tasks were assigned to each team member to research and develop a prototype for one of the potential designs. The group also agreed on a deadline of May 5th for the completion of the prototypes and to reconvene on May 10th to review the prototypes and make a final decision on the design to move forward with. John raised a concern about potential copyright issues with some of the design ideas, so the team agreed to research and ensure that all designs were original and did not infringe on any existing patents or trademarks. Additionally, Jane suggested creating a shared document to document the design process and progress, and the team agreed to use Google Docs for this purpose. Finally, Tom expressed concern about the tight deadline for completing the prototypes, so the group discussed the feasibility of the deadline and agreed to revisit it if necessary after the initial research phase.
